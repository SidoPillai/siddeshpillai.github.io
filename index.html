About Me

Hello friend! My name is Siddesh Pillai. I live in Rochester, New York. I am highly motivated indie developer, Computer Science graduate student at Rochester Institute of Technology. I am always learning new things in quest for knowledge. I have published apps, contributed to the open source and worked on scalable technologies.

I have interned as a Product Engineer at Esri Inc. where I had the privilege to work with the Android Runtime Core and Qt Core teams and contributed new features to the Android SDK and Qt SDK during my 7 month tenure and also as a Software Engineer at Intuit Inc. where I worked with the DevOps team and contributed to the build the backend for Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment platform.

Last fall, I worked as a Research Assistant at my university, where I developed an end to end android application VuraWiga, a tool designed for doctors in Africa to create awareness about better medical practices in collaboration with University of Rwanda. VuraWiga has been supported by Grand Challenges Canada. Grand Challenges Canada is funded by the Government of Canada and is dedicated to supporting Bold Ideas with Big Impact in global health.
